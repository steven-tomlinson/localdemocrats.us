Modern Infrastructure for Local Democratic Collaboration

LocalDemocrats.US is a public-interest Political Action Committee building a shared national directory and data platform for Democratic Party central committees — designed for transparency, accountability, and public trust.

Launched with $0 cash

This PAC is founded entirely on in-kind contributions of time, software, and technical infrastructure. No corporate funding. No dark money. Just open systems and public accountability.

Our Mission

To strengthen local Democratic organizations by providing a shared, transparent, and accessible data environment — enabling collaboration, institutional memory, and public-facing accountability using modern technology.

National Directory

A continuously maintained directory of Democratic Party central committees, officers, bylaws, and public contacts.

Open Data Explorer

A public-facing data explorer enabling citizens, activists, and journalists to understand party structure and governance.

Transparency by Design

Architecture designed for auditability, provenance, and eventual integration with distributed ledger technologies.

Local Control

Data is stewarded in collaboration with local committees — not centralized for control, but federated for trust.

Why This Matters

Local party organizations are the backbone of democratic participation, yet their records, leadership structures, and institutional knowledge are often fragmented, inaccessible, or lost.

LocalDemocrats.US exists to modernize that infrastructure — in the open — so trust is earned through visibility, not rhetoric.
